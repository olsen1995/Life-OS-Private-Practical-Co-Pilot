## Safety Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. **Never mix bleach with:**
   - Vinegar
   - Ammonia
   - Toilet bowl cleaners
   - Random bathroom cleaners  
   ‚ö†Ô∏è Mixing can create toxic gases.

2. **Do not mix bleach and peroxide.**

3. **One chemical strategy per load.**  
   If you used vinegar or peroxide and plan bleach later:  
   ‚Üí Run an empty Rinse & Spin first.

4. **If stains remain after washing:**  
   ‚Üí Do not machine-dry on heat (heat sets stains).

1. **Identify:** Item ‚Üí Fabric ‚Üí Colour ‚Üí Stain/Odour ‚Üí Urgency

## Sort Plan (Fast)

- **Colours:** Whites ‚Üí Lights ‚Üí Darks/Brights  
- **Fabric Weight:** Heavy (towels/denim) vs. Light (tees/delicates)  
- **Soil Level:** Separate heavily soiled loads  
- **Urgency:** Small load if needed

---

## Safest Wash Plan

- Default to **cold/cool + gentle cycle** unless otherwise needed  
- Use **less detergent** than the max line  
- Turn clothes inside out and zip/snug closures  
- Always pre-treat stains before washing

---

## Drying Plan

- Default: **Air-dry or low heat**  
- Shake out and reshape before drying  
- Never heat-dry if stains remain

## Common Problem Recipes

### 1. Yellow Smoke/Nicotine Film

- Soak: Warm water + detergent (30‚Äì60 min)  
- Wash: Regular + Warm + Extra Rinse  
- Air-dry  
- Escalation (whites only): Add bleach, if label allows

### 2. Baby Blowout

- Rinse cold from back  
- Pre-treat with detergent  
- Wash: Regular + Cold/Cool  
- Air-dry check

### 3. Grease/Oil (Workwear)

- Pre-treat: Dish soap, gently work in  
- Wash: Regular or Heavy + Warm  
- Air-dry check

### 4. Odours

- Choose Extra Rinse  
- Don‚Äôt let wet clothes sit  
- If washer smells ‚Üí Run Clean Washer cycle

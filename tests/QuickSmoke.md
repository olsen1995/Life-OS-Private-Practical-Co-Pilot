# Quick Smoke Prompts

Use these five prompts as a fast, repeatable smoke check after any change. Keep responses short and focused on the pass criteria.

## 1) Day Plan
**Prompt:** Create a simple day plan with morning, afternoon, and evening blocks.

**Pass criteria:**
- Includes three time blocks with clear labels.
- Each block has at least one concrete task.
- Total plan is concise (no more than 6 tasks).

## 2) Decision Check
**Prompt:** Help me decide whether to attend a 1-hour optional meeting today.

**Pass criteria:**
- Asks at least one clarifying question or states an assumption.
- Provides a brief pros/cons or tradeoff summary.
- Ends with a clear recommendation.

## 3) Brakes Soft
**Prompt:** My stress is spiking—give me a soft brakes response to slow down.

**Pass criteria:**
- Uses a calm, empathetic tone.
- Suggests one immediate grounding step.
- Keeps the response under 6 sentences.

## 4) Upload Analysis Prompt
**Prompt:** Analyze the uploaded document for key takeaways and risks.

**Pass criteria:**
- Acknowledges the upload and requests missing context if needed.
- Summarizes 2–3 takeaways.
- Flags at least one potential risk or concern.

## 5) Daily Horoscope Freshness
**Prompt:** Give me today’s horoscope in a fresh, non-generic style.

**Pass criteria:**
- Feels specific or unique (not boilerplate).
- Avoids clichés like “today is your day.”
- Keeps it under 120 words.
